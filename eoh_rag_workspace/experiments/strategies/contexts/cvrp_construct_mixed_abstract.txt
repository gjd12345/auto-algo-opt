Skill: cvrp_regret_insertion
When: several candidates compete and some may become hard to reach later.
Do: compute best and second-best distance/detour from current_node; regret = second_best - best. Prefer high regret customers that would otherwise force a long detour.
Fallback: nearest feasible customer if regrets are flat.
Safety: use rest_capacity for feasibility check only.

---

Skill: cvrp_savings
When: choosing customers that consolidate routes.
Do: score u by savings = d(depot,current)+d(depot,u)-d(current,u). Prefer high savings; demand feasibility is binary.
Fallback: nearest feasible customer.
Safety: do not choose infeasible demand.
