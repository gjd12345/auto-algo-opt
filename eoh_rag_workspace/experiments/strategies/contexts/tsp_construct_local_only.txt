Skill: tsp_regret_insertion
When: several candidate nodes compete and one may become costly later.
Do: for each candidate, compare best and second-best local connection alternatives; regret = second_best - best. Prefer high regret with moderate current distance.
Fallback: nearest neighbor if regret is flat.
Safety: avoid O(n^3) loops.

---

Skill: tsp_nearest_insertion
When: nearest neighbor may create long final edges.
Do: score candidate u by distance current->u plus u->destination, favoring nodes that are close to both current path and return direction.
Fallback: use nearest neighbor if scores tie.
Safety: compute only over unvisited_nodes.

---

Skill: tsp_farthest_insertion
When: distant clusters may be left until too late.
Do: prefer candidate with larger distance from destination or current cluster, then choose the least damaging such node. Seed far regions early.
Fallback: nearest neighbor for small remaining sets.
Safety: apply only to unvisited_nodes.

---

Skill: tsp_two_opt_awareness
When: a nearest step may create crossing or long edges.
Do: penalize candidates that create very long current->u edge relative to local neighbor distances; prefer choices that keep route locally smooth.
Fallback: nearest neighbor.
Safety: selector remains greedy and bounded.
