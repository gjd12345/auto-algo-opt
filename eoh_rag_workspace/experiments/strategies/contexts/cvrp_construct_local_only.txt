Skill: cvrp_regret_insertion
When: several candidates compete and some may become hard to reach later.
Do: compute best and second-best distance/detour from current_node; regret = second_best - best. Prefer high regret customers that would otherwise force a long detour.
Fallback: nearest feasible customer if regrets are flat.
Safety: use rest_capacity for feasibility check only.

---

Skill: cvrp_savings
When: choosing customers that consolidate routes.
Do: score u by savings = d(depot,current)+d(depot,u)-d(current,u). Prefer high savings; demand feasibility is binary.
Fallback: nearest feasible customer.
Safety: do not choose infeasible demand.

---

Skill: cvrp_far_first
When: near depot or starting a new route.
Do: prefer distant unvisited customers to seed routes toward clusters far from depot. Use distance_matrix[depot][u] to measure distance from depot; prefer larger values early in the route, then switch to nearest once away from depot.
Fallback: nearest feasible customer if far nodes exhausted.
Safety: never select customer with demand > rest_capacity.

---

Skill: cvrp_sweep
When: customers are spatially clustered around depot.
Do: prefer nodes with similar polar angle from depot as current_node, then nearest distance; this keeps routes in angular sectors.
Fallback: nearest capacity if angle is unavailable.
Safety: use deterministic atan2 scoring only.
