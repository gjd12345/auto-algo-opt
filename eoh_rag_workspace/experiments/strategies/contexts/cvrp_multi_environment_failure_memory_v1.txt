AGENT MEMORY — development evidence, not held-out evidence.

The confirmed n2 parent improved across earlier fresh uniform, clustered, and rectangular environments. A later density-aware structure improved new uniform 50 and clustered 100 instances, but regressed on rectangular 200 instances and failed the overall paired gate (44 wins, 46 losses). This is an environment tradeoff, not a successful discovery.

Preserve the confirmed n2 backbone. Read the numeric search and confirmation objectives for all three environments before modifying it. Make one structural change whose mechanism can remain useful across geometries and scales. Do not repair the rejected density candidate, do not copy its empty-neighbor edge case, and do not optimize only the currently worst environment by sacrificing another one.
