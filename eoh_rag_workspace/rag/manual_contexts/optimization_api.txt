Optimization API rules:
- Preserve the ship-id multiset: do not drop, duplicate, or invent ships.
- A trial move must save both Assign states before RemoveShip/AddShip.
- After a trial AddShip succeeds, call GenRoute and compare cost_delta.
- If a move is rejected or infeasible, restore both Assigns before the next trial.
- Commit at most one best move per call when unsure; simple safe moves beat complex invalid moves.
- Call dispatch.RenewnTotalCost exactly once before return.
